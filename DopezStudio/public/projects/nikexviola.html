Video Ad

Production

NIKE x Viola Hoffman

Background

Nike, the world-renowned sports brand, teamed up with popular blogger Viola Hoffman to create a unique advertisement campaign. The aim was to promote their latest line of athletic gear using Viola's substantial online influence and unique content creation style.

Production

The first step involved a series of meeting between the nike team and viola to align their goals and visions for the campaign. They decided on the message, the target audience, and the overall aesthetic of the video.

Video Shooting

The video shooting took place in various urban and outdoor locations that resonated with the sporty and adventurous image of Nike, Viola known for her dunamic and energetic style on screen presence, brought those elements into the shoot. The team captured different scenarios featuring Viola using the Nike Products in her daily workout routine

Post-Production

In Post productio, the footage was edited and polished to match Nike's hight quality standard.The editing process emphasized highlighting the product's features and Viola's geniune interaction with them. Sound, mixing , color and lighting were carefully considered to create a cohesive and visually appealing final result.

This case study stands as the testemant to the power of combining influencers marketing with high quality video production.

Outcome

The resulting video was a blend of Violas energeting approach and Nike's brand image. The campaign was highly successful, leading to an increase in brand engagement and product sales
